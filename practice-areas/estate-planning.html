Area of Practice | Estate Planning

In Florida, the creation of a will is still a very formal process and certain requirements must be met in order for your will to be respected and accepted by the court. At Marsicano + Leyva PLLC, our attorneys believe in providing the highest quality of service to all clients, whether their matter involves the crafting of a simple will or a more complicated inheritance scheme.

    Our firm provides a full range of estate planning services including:
  • Wills
  • Trusts
  • Living wills
  • Asset protection strategies
  • Durable powers of attorney
  • Designation of a health care surrogate
  • Designation of a pre-need guardian for yourself and children
  • Business successions

A well-crafted estate plan can provide security for your loved ones; protection for you in the event you are injured or suffer a debilitating illness; and ultimately it provides an individual with all around peace of mind.



At Marsicano + Leyva PLLC we recognize that it can be difficult to navigate the many options available for planning your estate, which can encompass wills and trusts in all their varied forms. Marsicano + Leyva PLLC is dedicated to providing its clients with candid, straightforward, and thoughtful estate planning advice. We will work with you to determine which estate planning option is best suited for your needs.
