Family Law

Marsicano + Leyva PLLC is a full service Family Law Firm focused on catering to its clients during what is undeniably a complex and emotionally demanding chapter in their lives. Whether you need a divorce, adoption, annulment, enforcement/modification as it pertains to children and alimony, child custody, prenuptial agreements or postnuptial agreements, Marsicano + Leyva PLLC will handle your case with the skill, care and integrity necessary to advocate for your legal rights.

Business Law

Marsicano + Leyva PLLC is dedicated to providing its business, franchise, and startup entrepreneurial clients with valuable legal guidance and representation on issues prevalent throughout the life of the company, from formation to wind-down. Whether it’s a routine contract or complex transaction, Marsicano + Leyva PLLC will work with you to increase your company’s overall performance and bottom line. Aside from a traditional hourly fee structure, Marsicano + Leyva PLLC offers a variety of unbundled legal services at a flat rate.

Real Estate Law

Marsicano + Leyva PLLC is dedicated to providing clients and Homeowners' Associations with comprehensive assistance in a variety of areas involving Florida real property including the rights and interests in property, sales, purchases and other transfers of real property, settlement of claims against property rights or other dealings involving Florida commercial and residential real estate. Contracts related to real estate can be confusing; our attorneys will sit down with you and explain every aspect of your case. Whether it involves contract analysis and interpretation, litigation strategy or help you with the many different aspects of a purchase, sale or lease of real property, rely on our experienced team to assist you.

Commercial Litigation

Marsicano + Leyva PLLC endeavors to insulate its corporate clients from liability. However, when a lawsuit is unavoidable, the attorneys at Marsicano + Leyva PLLC assist clients in making well-informed, strategic decisions from the onset of a dispute through resolution. We are experienced in handling a wide range of commercial disputes in both federal and state court including breach of contract cases, intellectual property disputes, partnership/joint venture disputes, business torts, breach of fiduciary duty allegations, and shareholder issues, among other areas. Above all, Marsicano + Leyva PLLC strives to execute strategies that match your company's objectives in an effective and efficient manner.

Civil Litigation

Our attorneys have experience both in and out of the court room and are familiar with all areas of civil litigation, including motion practice, depositions, in-depth discovery, negotiation, mediation, trial preparation, and appeals. Whether its defending you in a dispute or pursuing a claim, let the attorneys at Marsicano + Leyva PLLC put their experience to work for you.

Consumer Litigation

Our attorneys are passionate about defending you from predatory and aggressive behavior stemming from unethical debt collection practices. If you are being harassed regarding your debt, you may be entitled to compensation under the Telephone Consumer Protection Act, the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act, the Florida Consumer Collection Practices Act, the Fair Credit Reporting Act, and the Florida Deceptive and Unfair Trade Practices Act. Whether its foreclosure or credit card defense, our attorneys have experience on both sides of the issues enabling them to provide you with valuable and realistic insight into your case.

Estate Planning

In Florida, the creation of a will is still a very formal process and certain requirements must be met in order for your will to be respected by a court. A well-crafted estate plan can provide protection for your family, protection for you in the event you are injured or suffer an illness, and ultimately provide you with all around peace of mind. At Marsicano + Leyva PLLC we recognize that it can be difficult to navigate the many options available for planning your estate, which can encompass wills and trusts in all their varied forms. Marsicano + Leyva PLLC is dedicated to providing its clients with candid, straightforward, and thoughtful estate planning advice. We will work with you to determine which estate planning option is best suited for your needs.

Probate

At Marsicano + Leyva PLLC, our attorneys believe in providing the highest quality of service to all clients, whether their matter involves the crafting of a simple will or contesting a complicated inheritance. Our goal is to provide clients with exceptional personalized service with low overhead cost. Marsicano + Leyva PLLC understands that legal proceedings and probate matters can often seem unavoidably complex and confusing. If you have questions or concerns relating to a will, living will, power of attorney or probate matter do not hesitate to contact us and set up your free consultation.

Employment Law

At Marsicano + Leyva PLLC, we understand the federal and state laws affecting the rights of both individuals and businesses in the employment context. We provide compassionate representation for employees whose rights have been violated and whose complaints have been ignored by their company’s human resource department. However, we also provide aggressive representation for employers (both small and large) who face accusations of illegal employment practices. Businesses can also take advantage of our guidance and advice to develop proactive policies and agreements that protect your company’s interests and decrease the risk of future litigation.
